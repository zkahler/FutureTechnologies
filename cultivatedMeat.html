What is Cultivated Meat?

Cultivated meat, also known as lab-grown or cell-based meat, is produced by cultivating animal cells in a controlled environment, rather than raising and slaughtering animals.

This innovative approach offers numerous benefits. It significantly reduces the environmental footprint, as it requires fewer resources like land and water, and emits lower greenhouse gases compared to traditional meat production. Cultivated meat also addresses animal welfare concerns by eliminating the need for factory farming and slaughter.

Additionally, it has the potential to provide a safer and more sustainable food supply by reducing the risk of foodborne illnesses and contamination. This technology represents a promising step towards a more ethical and sustainable future for meat consumption.
